GCC is back, this time with a Hackathon!

Join us as we build ideas, apps and community in Hyderabad! GCC Hack Day is a competition that aims to foster a stronger sense of community among women interested in computer science and provide a creative platform for innovation and entrepreneurship. Form a team, code for several hours consecutively, create something awesome, and present it to the judges to win prizes.

GCC's first annual hackathon, December 26th, 8:30 am to 6 pm at CHIREC International. We want to see you there!

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a hack day?

It is a chance for you to form a team, work on something you are passionate about, and turn it into a working prototype (known as a "hack")-- all in a span of few hours. Meet other women interested in technology, get guidance from top notch mentors, and win awesome prizes!

Why a hack day?

For the girls who attended our summer boot camps, we hope this will be an opportunity to leverage the skills they learnt to build something innovative. For the others, we hope this will will be a chance to learn and discover the endless possibilities of technology.

When, where and who?

The event is planned to be held on December 26, Saturday from 8:30am to 5:30pm at CHIREC International. We welcome all young women to participate, with or without programming experience!

See schedule below for more details.

What if I don't have a team or an idea?

No worries-- we've got your back! We’ve been working on some great activities to help you find teammates and brainstorm ideas.

What can I build?

Anything your heart desires! Web, mobile, gaming, education, an idea that sparked during the boot camps; you name it, we'll support it.

What if I don’t know how to code?

GCC Hack Day is the perfect time and place to learn. With the help of our great workshops, mentors and your own peers, you'll be in good shape!

What should I bring?

All you really need is your student ID. We provide the rest-- yummy food, take-aways, prizes and a heap load of fun!

How much does it cost?

GCC Hack Day is free for all registered hackers. We provide meals, workspace, computers and WiFi for the event!

I have another question...

We're here for you. Email us at girlscodecamp@gmail.com or message us on Facebook!
